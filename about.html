Day 31 – Returning to Paper

June 23, 2025

It’s been a month since I began the digital detox. My world has slowed, softened. I go to libraries now. I journal with ink, stretch with yoga, and smell pages like perfume. The silence feels alive. I don’t miss social media — not even a little.

History is Healing

June 22, 2025

Reading about the Katipunan — the fire that stirred the Philippine revolution — connects me to something deeper. And then, Japan’s Heian court, their lantern-lit literature... It’s all a thread in who I am, who I’m becoming. Studying makes me feel whole.
