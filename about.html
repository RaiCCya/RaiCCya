Meet Rai

Hello. I’m Rai — a journal keeper, word collector, and quiet wanderer in the realms of language. This space is where I gather thoughts like pebbles and pin light to paper lanterns.

My study is handwritten, unhurried. Japanese and Filipino words fill the margins. I learn by living slowly, by being present. This site reflects that: a little archive of clarity, curiosity, and calm.

Whether you're learning or resting, you're always welcome here.
